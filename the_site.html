About This Site


I built this place entirely from scratch using HTML, CSS, and Javascript.
Here, you can see my story and work, as well as contact me and in general see what kind of designs I can create.
This site is very much a work in progress right now, so you may see some goofy stuff going on, but that is perfectly fine.
I believe strongly that good design can be achieved without using bulky frameworks like wordpress. I don't doubt they can be highly useful, but I prefer to not use them.
